Our Story

Jane and John met while studying culinary arts in Bhutan. Their shared passion for food and commitment to promoting sustainable dining led them to open this restaurant. Over the years, they have perfected their craft, blending traditional Bhutanese recipes with contemporary techniques. Their goal is to make every meal a memorable experience for their guests.
